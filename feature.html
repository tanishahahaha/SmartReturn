Smart Return

  • 1.Income Tax Calculator

    Estimates income tax liability based on earnings, deductions, and tax slabs. Users can input salary, investments, and exemptions (80C, HRA, NPS) to compute taxes and optimize savings.

  • 2.Loan EMI Calculator

    Calculates monthly EMI payments for loans based on amount, interest rate, and tenure. Helps users plan home, car, personal, and business loans efficiently.

  • 3.SIP Suggestions

    Provides personalized mutual fund SIP recommendations based on financial goals, risk tolerance, and investment horizon. Helps users build long-term wealth.

  • 4.GST Calculator

    Computes GST (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) for businesses and individuals. Offers a breakdown of CGST, SGST, and IGST to determine tax-inclusive/exclusive prices.

  • 5.Tax Saving Investment

    Suggests investment options for tax deductions (80C, 80D, etc.), including ELSS, PPF, NPS, FDs, and insurance plans, helping users save on taxes while investing smartly.

  • 6.Future Tax Forecast

    Predicts future tax liability based on salary growth, investment trends, and tax law changes, enabling users to plan ahead for optimal tax efficiency.

  • 7.Investipedia

    A financial knowledge hub offering investment guides, tax policies, stock market insights, and wealth management tips to boost financial literacy.

  • 8.GST Toolkit

    Provides updated information on GST slabs for the current year, including applicable rates (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) for different goods/services, exemptions, and recent GST law amendments. Ideal for businesses and individuals looking to stay informed.
