Equity Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS) High Match

Lock-in Period: 3 years (shortest among tax-saving options)

Returns: Potentially 12-15% (market-linked)

Risk Level: Moderate to High

Maximum Deduction: ₹1,50,000 under Section 80C

ELSS funds invest primarily in equity markets, offering potential for capital appreciation while saving taxes.

Ideal for investors with a long-term horizon and moderate to high risk appetite.

Public Provident Fund (PPF) High Match

Lock-in Period: 15 years (can be extended)

Returns: Currently ~7.1% p.a. (government-backed)

Risk Level: Very Low (Government backed)

Maximum Deduction: ₹1,50,000 under Section 80C

One of the safest tax-saving instruments with tax-free returns.

Annual contribution limit: ₹1.5 lakhs with partial withdrawal facility after 7 years.

National Pension System (NPS) High Match

Lock-in Period: Until retirement (60 years)

Returns: 8-10% historical average

Risk Level: Low to Moderate (depends on allocation)

Maximum Deduction: ₹1,50,000 under 80C + Additional ₹50,000 under 80CCD(1B)

Offers dual tax benefits - deduction under Section 80C and additional ₹50,000 under Section 80CCD(1B).

Flexible asset allocation between equity, corporate debt, government securities, and alternative investments.

Tax-Saving Fixed Deposit Medium Match

Lock-in Period: 5 years

Returns: ~5.5-7% p.a. (varies by bank)

Risk Level: Very Low

Maximum Deduction: ₹1,50,000 under Section 80C

Guaranteed returns with bank deposits up to ₹5 lakhs insured by DICGC.

Simple investment process with most banks offering online application.

Unit Linked Insurance Plan (ULIP) Medium Match

Lock-in Period: 5 years

Returns: Variable (market-linked)

Risk Level: Moderate to High

Maximum Deduction: ₹1,50,000 under Section 80C

Combination of insurance and investment with tax-free maturity proceeds under Section 10(10D).

Offers flexibility to switch between funds based on market conditions.

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY) Low Match

Lock-in Period: Until girl child turns 21

Returns: Currently ~7.6% p.a. (government-backed)

Risk Level: Very Low (Government backed)

Maximum Deduction: ₹1,50,000 under Section 80C

Specially designed for parents of girl children below 10 years of age.

One of the highest interest rates among government schemes with tax-exempt interest and maturity amount.
