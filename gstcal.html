Understanding GST in India

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. It replaced multiple taxes like VAT, Service Tax, and Excise Duty to create a unified tax structure.

Current GST Rates in India

Rate Applicable for
0% Essential goods like fresh fruits, vegetables, milk
5% Essential commodities, packaged food items
12% Computers, processed food, mobiles
18% Most goods and services, electronics
28% Luxury items, automobiles, tobacco products

How We Calculate GST

Our calculator uses the following formula to determine GST:

GST Amount = Original Amount × GST Rate ÷ 100

Total Amount = Original Amount + GST Amount
